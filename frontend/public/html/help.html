Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Payment System?

A payment system is a mechanism that enables the transfer of funds between individuals or entities. It facilitates transactions for goods and services, typically involving the exchange of money, electronic funds, or digital currencies.

How Secure is Your Payment System?

Security is our top priority. We employ robust encryption protocols and stringent security measures to protect user information and financial transactions. Our system undergoes regular audits and updates to ensure the highest level of security.

What Are Voice Passwords and How Are They Created?

Voice passwords are a unique authentication method that uses the characteristics of a person's voice for verification. Here's how they are created:

  1. Type in your password At the time of registeration, you are prompted to enter in a 4 digit password.
  2. Algorithmic Calculation: Using advanced algorithms, your password is transformed into a secure voice password.
  3. Test and Save: Your voice password is told to you at the time of creation, which can further be used anytime you wish to make a payment.

Benefits of Voice Passwords

  • Security: Voice passwords are difficult to replicate, providing enhanced security.
  • Convenience: Access your account using your voice, eliminating the need to remember complex passwords.
  • Accessibility: Especially beneficial for visually impaired users.
